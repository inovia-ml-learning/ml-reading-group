Inovia Machine Learning Reading Group

Inovia's weekly ML Reading Group! Join us for fun, high-level tutorials on the ML ecosystem from experienced researchers and developers in industry and academia, as well as discussion on future outlooks of ML. Our guest list features folks from across the ML stack, from FPGA design to virtual cell foundation models, providing for a holistic learning experience. We are fortunate to be joined by a great group of speakers from industry and academia, including from places like NVIDIA, Cursor, OpenAI, Thrive, The Vector Institute, and more!

Format

The goal of each session is to introduce, educate, and discuss a topic in ML at a very high level. Each lecture is designed to build upon the last, but please do not feel pressured to follow along with every single lecture. The lectures are also designed to stand relatively independently for non-technical or beginner audiences, so you can watch them in any order. The suggested format:

  • Tutorial (~20 minutes) — context, intuition, and the key technical ideas.
  • Audience questions (~10 minutes).
  • Open discussion — limitations, future directions, and how the topic fits into the broader ML stack.

How do I get involved?

Want to present? Email the organizers or open an issue on the repo with a topic and a short blurb. In-progress work and open questions are welcome.
